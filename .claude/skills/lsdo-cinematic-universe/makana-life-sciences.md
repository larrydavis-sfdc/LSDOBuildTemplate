# Makana Life Sciences

Part of the [Makana MedTech Cinematic Universe](makana-medtech-cinematic-universe.md).

Makana Life Sciences is a global life sciences company founded in 1886, headquartered in Honolulu, Hawaii, with operations in more than 60 countries. The name "Makana" means "gift" in Hawaiian, reflecting the company's mission to deliver innovative healthcare solutions as a gift to humanity. Makana operates through two divisions: **Makana Pharmaceuticals** and **Makana MedTech**.

For the full MedTech product catalog, see [Makana MedTech Products](makana-medtech-products.md). For demo personas, see [Makana MedTech Personas](makana-medtech-personas.md). For customer accounts, see [Makana Accounts](makana-accounts.md). For the primary competitor, see [Konkurrent Medical](makana-medtech-competitor-konkurrent.md).

## Makana Pharmaceuticals

Research-driven division developing and manufacturing prescription medications. Emphasizes targeted innovation and personalized medicine. Maintains a global network of R&D centers and actively partners with biotech startups and academic institutions to accelerate drug discovery.

### Therapeutic Areas

**Diabetes Care**
Oral and injectable therapies for Type 1 and Type 2 diabetes management, including combination therapies and glucose-lowering agents. Focus on the full spectrum of diabetes care from early intervention through complication prevention.

**Cardiovascular**
Antihypertensives, anticoagulants, lipid-lowering agents, anti-thrombotics, and therapies for heart failure. Emphasis on understanding the interplay between metabolic and cardiovascular health.

**Neuroscience**
Treatments for multiple sclerosis, Alzheimer's disease, Parkinson's disease, epilepsy, depression, anxiety disorders, and pain management. Research emphasizes understanding underlying disease mechanisms to develop targeted, effective treatments.

**Ophthalmology**
Specialized focus on diabetic retinopathy and macular degeneration. Portfolio includes injectable biologics and sustained-release implants. Works closely with MedTech Vision unit for comprehensive eye care solutions.

**Pulmonary Hypertension**
Oral, inhaled, and IV treatments targeting both arterial and venous forms of pulmonary hypertension. One of the most robust portfolios in this therapeutic area.

## Makana MedTech

Engineers and manufactures medical devices and technologies across interventional and surgical domains. Emphasizes interoperability and connected care, integrating devices with cloud-based platforms to deliver actionable insights. Frequently collaborates with Makana Pharmaceuticals to offer comprehensive patient solutions.

Products are documented in detail in the [MedTech Product Catalog](makana-medtech-products.md). The sales team operates within the [MedTech Territory Model](../managing-medtech-territories/SKILL.md).

### Business Units

**Cardiovascular**
Interventional cardiology tools including stents, catheters, heart valves, and imaging systems. Cardiac rhythm management devices including pacemakers. Structural heart innovations and wearable heart rhythm monitors.

**Electrophysiology**
Diagnostic and treatment tools for heart rhythm disorders. Cardiac ablation systems, mapping technologies, and monitoring systems for conditions like atrial fibrillation and ventricular tachycardia.

**Orthopedics**
Joint reconstruction systems (hip, knee, shoulder), trauma implants, and spinal technologies. Invests in innovative materials, digital surgery planning tools, and AI-assisted intraoperative solutions.

**Surgical**
Minimally invasive instruments, endoscopy platforms, and surgical robotics. Advanced wound closure systems, hemostats, and energy devices for vascular and general surgery. Includes the [Aurora Surgical Robot](makana-medtech-products.md#Aurora%20Surgical%20Robot) platform.

**Vision**
Intraocular lenses, cataract and refractive surgery platforms, and surgical visualization systems. Diagnostic imaging for ophthalmology. Devices for treatment and management of diabetic eye diseases, complementing Makana Pharmaceuticals' ophthalmology therapies.

**Diabetes Management**
Continuous glucose monitoring systems, insulin pumps, and blood glucose meters. Focus on empowering individuals with diabetes through connected device ecosystems and data-driven care. Includes the [Makana Sense Monitor](makana-medtech-products.md#Diabetes%20/%20DME%20(Durable%20Medical%20Equipment)).

**Neuroscience**
Neuromodulation devices for pain management, deep brain stimulation systems for Parkinson's disease, and neurostimulation platforms. Complements Makana Pharmaceuticals' neuroscience research with device-based treatment options.

**Pulmonary Hypertension**
Specialized catheters and monitoring systems for diagnosis and management of pulmonary hypertension. Works alongside Makana Pharmaceuticals' therapy portfolio.

## Cross-Division Alignment

Makana's two divisions are deliberately aligned by therapeutic area. For each major disease area, Makana can offer both pharmaceutical treatments and device-based solutions:

| Therapeutic Area | Pharma | MedTech |
|-----------------|--------|---------|
| Diabetes | Oral and injectable therapies | CGMs, insulin pumps, glucose meters |
| Cardiovascular | Antihypertensives, anticoagulants | Stents, heart valves, catheters |
| Neuroscience | MS, Alzheimer's, Parkinson's therapies | Neuromodulation, deep brain stimulation |
| Ophthalmology | Biologics, sustained-release implants | IOLs, surgical platforms, diagnostics |
| Pulmonary Hypertension | Oral, inhaled, IV treatments | Diagnostic catheters, monitoring |

## Regulatory Framework

Makana's products undergo rigorous regulatory scrutiny:
- Pharmaceutical products require New Drug Applications (NDAs) through the FDA
- Medical devices receive clearance through 510(k) submissions or Premarket Approval (PMA) applications
- Makana maintains dedicated regulatory affairs teams per division and per major geography
